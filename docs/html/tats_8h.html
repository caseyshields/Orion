◆ TATS_CELESTIAL_SPHERE_RADIUS

#define TATS_CELESTIAL_SPHERE_RADIUS   (1<<31)

2^31/256 meters, or half the maximum distance representable by TATS((2^32)/256 = 16777216 meters). Novas uses Astronomical units for distance and they set their celestial sphere(for the purposes of parallax) at 2*10^14 AU
